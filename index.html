Bloomington Hackclub


Hello!
Enjoy programming?
Or PCB(Circuit Board) design?
Or testing other people's things?
Or haven't ever done any of these things, but want to?
Then you've come to the right place! We enjoy all of these things, and can teach you! We meet after school from 2:40-4:30 on Tuesdays and Thursdays, in room 102(Ms. Peterson's Room) and although we are pretty free, we have some rules:

You CAN: Code, design circuit boards, design 3D objects to get 3D printed(or not), learn any of those from a tutorial or another person, test someone else's or your own game/app/circuit board, work on group projects

You can NOT: Generically play games that haven't been made by someone who has ASKED you to playtest their game, watch youtube videos, try to break ChromeOS or other district software (yes I know it's fun but we don't want the district to shut us down), etc.

Arthur and Nadia get final say on any decision, and if something is allowed or not!
